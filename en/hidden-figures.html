Hidden Figures

By Charlotte Mettetal, published le 10/20/2025 at 11:18 a.m

Discover or rediscover Hidden Figures

Hidden Figures is an american biographical movie directed by Theodore Melfi in 2016. This movie tells the story of three African-American women : Katherine Johnson, a mathematician, Dorothy Vaughan, a computer scientist and Mary Jackson an aeronautical engineer.

"Three African-American women : Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan and Mary Jackson."

Who are they ?

These three women worked at NASA during the period of segregation and the Cold War.This film is based on a true story and shows the work that these three women did for this agency. This movie depicts sereval key moments in the lives of these women.
Katherine Jonhson managed to calculate the trajectories for the Mercury program and the Apollo 11 mission.

Katherine Johnson

Dorothy Vaughan became head of the computing departement.

Dorothy Vaughan

Finally, Mary Jackson became the first Africain-American aeronautical engineer.

Mary Jackson

A socially conscious movie

This movie highlights how these black women were not immediately recognized as competent scientists because of their skin color and gender.
The movie is an adaptation of the book of the same name, written by Margot Lee Shetterly in 2016.
