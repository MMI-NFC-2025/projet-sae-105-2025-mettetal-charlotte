Mileva Einstein : Matilda effect or Matthew effect ?

There is one woman who does not appear on this timeline. She is Mileva Einstein , Albert Einstein's first wife.
Her case is controvesrial. Mileva worked with her husband on various projects. However, we cannot know if she is a victim of the Matilda effect or the Matthew effect (not being recognized because the colleage with whom the scientist works is better known).
