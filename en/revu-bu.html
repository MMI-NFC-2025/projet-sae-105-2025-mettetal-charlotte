An item from the university library

By Charlotte Mettetal, published le 10/20/2025 at 11:30 am

Discover the magazine

The item from the university library that I have chosen to present is a magazine entitled Les aventurières : elles ont bravé les dangers et brisé les stéréotypes by Les Grands Dossiers Sciences Humaines.
This magazine recounts the stories of remarkables women who have been forgotten or remaine unknown.

Magazine

"The storie of remarkables women who have been forgotten or remaine unknown."

What is it about ?

This magazine highlights the courage, heroism and achievements of women. It also recounts how women defied the stereotypes of the eras to which they belonged. These eras were characterized by important place men held in society and in women's lives. These women stood out by proving that they were as valuable as men and that they did not need them to live.

"The courage, heroism and achievements of women"

Who can we find ?

In this magazine, we find women such as Anne Bonny and Mary Read, two female pirates sentenced to death by hanging.
Amelia Earhart, an aviator who achived the feat of flying solo across the Atlantic. She desappeared in mid-flight in 1937.
Germaine Tillion, an ethnologist renowned for her knowledge of the terrain in Algeria. She was involved in the resistance during World War II.
