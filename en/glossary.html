Glossary

You can find all the most complex words here

  • Atomic nucleus: region located at the center of an atom. It consists of protons and netrons.

  • Chain reaction : set of phenomena triggered by one another.

  • Ethnologist : person who studies societies on all continents.

  • Isotope : each of the elements with the same atomic number but with different atomic masses.

  • Neutron: elementary particle that is electrically neutral.

  • Nobel Prize: established in 1901, it rewards individuals for their work in the service of humanity, thanks your their discoveries, actions, etc.

  • Nuclear cohesion : phenomenon that binds the elements of an atomic nucleus together.

  • Nuclear fission : splitting of a heavy atom into several lighter atoms during the collision between an atome and a neutron.

  • Nuclear physics : science that studies all physical phenomena involving the atomic nucleus.

  • Particle accelerator : produces and accelerates charged particles.

  • Polonium : an atomic element whose isotopes are all radiactive.

  • Protactinium : radioactive element.

  • Radioactivity : atomic nuclei can transform themselves by emitting types of radiaction.

  • Radium : a highly radioactive chemical element. It belongs to the uraniumn family.

  • Segregation : imposed separation of a social group in terms of rights or activities.

  • Social constraints : obstacles to freedom of action that originate in the structures, laws, customs and practices of a society.

  • Uranium : natural radioactive element.
